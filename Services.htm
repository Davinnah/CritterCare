Services
Included
All Critter Care visits include:
  • Fresh water and owner approved treats.
  • Observation of you pet's health, safety and wellbeing.
  • Lots of play and individual attention.
For services 2 days or longer
  • Bringing in mail, newspapers, and packages
  • Trash/Recycle bins in/out
  • Watering plants
  • Rotating lights/blinds
  • Checking doors/windows
Critter Care will provide care for a wide range of pets: mammals, reptiles, amphibians, and birds.  
Consultation
  • A consultaion should occur prior the first service provided by Critter Care.  Another consultation should occur if the needs of your pet(s) change.
  • This meeting gives you & your pet(s) a chance to meet me.
  • We will also review your pet's needs along with accepted food & treats. A pet care sheet will be created at this time for my records. 
  • If Critter Care services are needed, an agreement will be signed at this time along with the delivering of any key(s) needed to grant acces to your pet(s).
  • Neighbor notification cards will also be provided if Critter Care services are attained.
Fee: Free
Basic 20 mins
Critter Care will come into your home, between 9am-7pm, to provide one of the following:
  • 1-2 Cats: feeding as instructed, clean litter & play time.
  • Small animal care: feeding as instructed, then clean cage/kennel.
  • 1-2 Dogs: feeding as instructed, fenced yard potty breaks only, playtime.  Additional dogs may be included for a negotiated price. 
Fee: $15
Dog Walk 20 mins
Critter Care will come into your home, between 9am-7pm, to feed & water as instructed, then take up to 2 dogs on a 20 minute leashed walk with waste removal.  Dogs must be leash trained. Additional dogs may be included for a negotiated price.
 
Fee: $15
Standard 30 mins
Critter care will come into your home, between 9am-7pm, to care to a variety of pets.  Ex: 2 dogs, 1 cat, and bird.
  • Short walk including waste removal for up to 2 dogs.  Additional dogs at a negotiated price
  • Litter cleaning 
  • Cage/Kennel maintenance for small animals/birds
Fee: $20
Extended 45 mins - 1 hr
Critter Care will come into your home, between 9am-7pm, to provide care to a variety of pets.  Ex: 2 dogs, 1 cat, and bird.
  • Longer walk including waste removal for up to 2 dogs.  Additional dogs at a negotiated price
  • Extra attention/playtime
  • Pet(s) with special needs
  • Litter cleaning 
  • Cage/Kennel maintenance for birds/small animals
Fee: $25-$30
Overnight 12 hrs (8pm - 8am)
Critter Care will come into your home to provide care to a variety of pets overnight. (8pm - 8am)   
  • Private walks including waste removal.
  • Extra attention/playtime
  • Pet(s) with special needs
  • Litter cleaning 
  • Cage/Kennel maintenance for birds/small animals
Fee: $55
Emergency

  • Appointments made with less than 24 hrs notice.
  • Appointments made outside normal daycare hours (9am - 7pm)
